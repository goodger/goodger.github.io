Japan is a wonderful country: exotic yet safe & clean, with friendly people, gorgeous views, and delicious food. The myriad differences from Western societies make Japan an essential travel destination, but they also make it a challenging place to visit.

Here are some quick tips to make your visit a success and as stress-free as possible.
